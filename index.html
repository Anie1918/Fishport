Fish Port

San Carlos, Tukuran, Zamboanga Del Sur

Welcome

Tukuran's fish port is a vital part of the local economy and a peaceful spot for visitors. Enjoy the long black sand beaches and a simple, abundant lifestyle.

About

  • Entrance: Free
  • Visitors: Locals and Tourists
  • Visiting Month: October - April

Safety & Rules

  • Watch children closely.
  • Swim only in allowed zones.
  • Don't swim after drinking alcohol.
  • Keep the area clean.
